# Anker USB-C Charger Buying Guide 2026

A practical reference for choosing the right Anker charger for phones, laptops, and tablets in 2026.

## Why USB-C PD Charging Matters

USB Power Delivery (PD) has replaced proprietary charging standards as the universal fast-charging protocol. A compliant charger negotiates the highest safe wattage your device supports automatically.

## Choosing the Right Wattage

For smartphones, a 20-30W USB-C PD charger is ideal. Tablets generally need 30-45W to charge at full speed. Laptops require at least 45W, with 65W or higher recommended for larger models. If you need to charge multiple devices simultaneously, a 65-100W GaN multi-port charger makes the most sense.

## GaN Technology Explained

Gallium Nitride (GaN) chargers run cooler, are physically smaller, and are more efficient than silicon-based chargers at equivalent wattage. In 2026, GaN is the default for any charger above 30W worth buying.
